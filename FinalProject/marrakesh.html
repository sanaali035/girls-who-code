Marrakesh, Morocco

About

Morocco is located in Africa, bordering the Atlantic Ocean and the Mediterranean Sea. Marrakesh is a former imperial city in western Morocco and a major economic center that is home to mosques, palaces and gardens. The medina is a densely packed, walled medieval city dating to the Berber Empire, with maze-like alleys, where thriving marketplaces sell traditional textiles, pottery and jewelry.
